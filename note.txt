================== jwt flow ==========================

[User Signup] --> [Server: Create User]
[User Login] --> [Server: Validate Credentials]
    |                      |
    v                      v
[Generate JWT] <-- [If valid]
    |
    v
[Send JWT to Client] --> [User: Store JWT]
    |
    v
[User: Access Protected Route] --> [Server: Verify Token]
    |
    v
[If valid] --> [Return Resource]
    |
[If invalid] --> [Return Unauthorized Error]
